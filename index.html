PPSS Index

+

Use the vertical sidebar to open each workspace. This index outlines how the ChatGPT-assisted Policy Planning Support System (PPSS) organizes collaboration, data, and governance.

+
+ +
+

Platform Overview

+

The PPSS platform coordinates co-design across policy planners, community partners, and AI copilots. It combines assisted briefing, persona management, scenario authoring, and transparent decision logs inspired by Sections 3 and 4 of the reference paper.

+
+ +
+

Core Screens

+
    +
  • Home: A shared dashboard for announcements, AI-generated briefs, and quick entry points into current missions.
    • +
  • Workspace: A co-creation board that surfaces personas, prompts AI ideation, and tracks design backlogs.
    • +
  • Report: A reporting hub that consolidates insights, governance checkpoints, and export-ready documents.
    • +
  • Settings: Administrative controls that configure roles, workflow policies, and assistant behaviors.
    • +
+
+ +
+

Suggested Flow

+
    +
  • Begin on Home to review a refreshed policy brief and commit to the collaboration cadence.
    • +
  • Move to Workspace to align personas, co-edit journeys, and iterate on AI-assisted scenarios.
    • +
  • Publish outcomes through Report, using PPSS templates that highlight deliberation history.
    • +
  • Return to Settings to fine-tune permissions or data integrations after each project milestone.
